Four directions for the aic mark.

aic reads a diff, drafts a conventional commit, ships it. The mark should read instantly at 16px (favicon / terminal title) and carry at 256px (hero). Direction 01 — Spark is the recommended primary: it is the clearest "AI" signal, scales the best, and drops the generic letter-in-a-box placeholder for something ownable.
